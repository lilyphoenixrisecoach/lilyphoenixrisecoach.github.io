Our free, confidential helpline is answered by professionally trained volunteers. So, whatever your concerns are, you can be rest assured that you will receive non-judgmental and non-critical listening. Please note, the caller's identity is never revealed and none of our calls are recorded or shared.

Lily Phoenix Rise Coaching is the only help line in Navi Mumbai and started functioning from 13 September 1998.

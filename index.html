EVENTS

Lily Phoenix Rise Coach organizes workshops on various levels for high-risk groups, such as school and college students, highly-stressed employees, and more. Our outreach programs aim to reach those struggling with chronic suffering or trauma, providing essential support and guidance.

GET HELP

24x7 Helpline: +91-9820466726
Contact us for support in emotional crises, trauma recovery, and mental health issues. Families experiencing trauma after a loved one's crisis are also encouraged to seek our help. We listen, we care.

VOLUNTEER

At Lily Phoenix Rise Coach, you can receive support from professionally trained volunteers. Selected for their empathy and understanding, each volunteer undergoes extensive training before assisting clients. Join us in making a difference in the lives of those affected by trauma and distress.

ABOUT US

Lily Phoenix Rise Coach is dedicated to providing holistic support for individuals dealing with trauma. Our mission is to offer compassionate care and confidential support, empowering clients to overcome their challenges. Learn more about us here.

BLOGS AND ARTICLES

Stay updated with the latest articles and blogs on trauma support and recovery. Learn about the prevalence of mental health issues, explore coping strategies, and read inspiring stories of resilience and hope from our community.
